FAQ

Answers before you ape in

Short, honest answers. No hype. No promises.

Is FlowScope giving financial advice?

No. FlowScope formats on-chain data (volume, liquidity, buy pressure, etc.) into readable posts. You stay responsible for your own trades.

Which chains are supported?

For now the bot focuses on Solana DEX pairs. More chains can be added later, but Solana is the main focus.

What is the difference between Free and PRO?

Free is a delayed preview with shorter summaries. PRO delivers earlier alerts with deeper metrics, stronger filters, and access to a private PRO channel - so you see it sooner.

How often are signals posted?

The scanner runs 24/7 and only posts when filters are passed. Some hours will be quiet, others more active - it depends on the market.

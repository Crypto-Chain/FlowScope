FAQ

Answers before you ape in

Short, honest answers. No hype. No promises.

Is FlowScope giving financial advice?

No. FlowScope formats on-chain data (volume, liquidity, buy pressure, etc.) into readable alerts. You always stay responsible for your own trades.

Which chains are supported?

For now the bot focuses on Solana DEX pairs. More chains can be added later, but Solana is the main playground.

What is the difference between Free and PRO?

Free shows selected signals with shorter summaries. PRO gets more metrics, faster alerts and access to a private PRO Telegram channel.

How often are signals posted?

The scanner runs continuously and only posts when filters are passed. Some hours will be quiet, others more active - it depends on the market.
